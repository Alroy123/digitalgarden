**Hope & Fear

Each player can hold a maximum of 3 Hope.

You, the DM, have a shared pool of Fear (max equal to the amount of players) 


**Gaining Hope

Everyone gains 1 Hope a long rest or Players earn Hope when they make the world better or overcome adversity. Or they can roll a duality dice (if they roll with Hope they get 2 Hope but if they roll with Fear the DM gets 1 Fear)

Hope is reset at the top of every session. 

  
**Heroic Moments: (Gain 2 Hope)
1. Saving another player.
2. Protecting an NPC.
3. Choosing the dangerous option for someone else's benefit.
4. Clears monster nests.
5. Helps a business.
6. Completes town projects

  
  

**Exploration: (Gain 1 Hope)
1. Explores a new area.
2. Discover ancient ruins.
3. Finds a hidden location.
4. Brings valuable information back to Evercrest.

**Personal Growth:
1. Whenever a player confronts part of their backstory. (Gain 2 Hope)  
2. Cool RP Moments: (anyone can give this out)
3. Gain 1 Hope a session (a player can only benefit from this once per session) 

**Spending Hope:

**Spend 1 Hope:
1. Gain Inspiration or Give Inspiration to another player. D12
2. Move anywhere in Initiative.
3. Advantage on a Jungle Hazard roll.
4. Reroll a failed check.
5. Ignore one level of Exhaustion until your next long rest.

**Spend 2 Hope:
1. Turn a hit into a critical hit.
2. Automatically succeed on a death save.
3. Immediately regain your movement.

**Spend 3 Hope:

  **The "Hero Moment."
1. Do something incredible. (This can be anything.)
Examples:
Stay standing at 1 HP. (But Dm gets 1 Fear)

Automatically succeed on a crucial skill check.

2. Inspire the entire party (everyone in the party except you gain one hope).
3. Regain 2D6 worth of spells (up to 6th level) or spent abilities 

**Gaining Fear:**

DM gets two Fear at the start of every session 

DM keeps any Fear not spent during a session

1. Entering the Jungle: Automatic 1 Fear.
2. Retreat: (gain 2 fear) 
3. Turning back before reaching the objective.
4. Downing a character: (gain 2 fear)
5. If they become lost in the jungle: (gain 1 Fear)

**Spending Fear (DM)

**Spend 1 Fear:
1. Give disadvantage on a Jungle Hazard roll.
2. The enemy gains advantage.
3. Reinforcements arrive.
4. The weather worsens. (Disadvantage on checks)
5. Interrupt a long rest

**Spend 2 Fear:
1. Trigger another Hazard roll.
2. A wandering monster appears.
3. The party becomes lost.
